About ArtOracle

Your gateway to discovering and purchasing unique art, with AI insights.

Frequently Asked Questions

ArtOracle is an online marketplace where artists can showcase their work and art lovers can buy unique pieces. Our platform uses AI technology to help estimate the price and value of each artwork.

Our AI algorithm analyzes various factors such as the artist's reputation, style, medium, and market trends to estimate the value of the artwork. This helps both buyers and artists make informed decisions.

To join ArtOracle as an artist, simply sign up on our platform and create your portfolio. Once approved, you can start listing your artworks and connect with buyers from around the world.

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods to make your transactions safe and convenient.

Our mission is to empower both emerging and established artists by giving them a platform to share their work and connect with a global audience. We aim to make art more accessible and help buyers discover unique pieces effortlessly.
